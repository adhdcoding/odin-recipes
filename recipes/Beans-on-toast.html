Beans on toast

A creamy and tasty cheesecake

I tend to make a larger cheesecake using twice the amount of Ingredients

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of white bread
  • half a tin of baked Beans
  • 50g of cheese

Steps:

  1. toast bread until golden brown
  2. Warm beans in pan on kitchen hob for 3-5 minutes until warm stiring constantly
  3. Grate cheese
  4. Slice toast into triangles and place on plate
  5. Pour beans onto toast and sprinkle with cheese to taste
